What is HackVerse?
HackVerse, an idea visualised by IE NITK, seeks to encourage enthusiastic minds to brainstorm on solutions for the most challenging issues in technical aspects. We provide a platform for innovations, where developers can test and showcase their potential to the best of their abilities.



About NITK
National Institute of Technology Karnataka or NITK is one of the premier engineering institutions of the country. Situated near Mangalore along the coast of the Arabian Sea, NITK is home to some of the brightest young minds of the country engaged in a wide array of disciplines. With a vibrant and robust student body, NITK has been the launchpad for people with great ideas that have grown to be very successful ventures including Practo, Purpule, SaveMonk among others. This year, we celebrate our Diamond Jubilee. To commemorate 60 years of NITK, IE NITK aims to provide students throughout the country an opportunity to do the same with Hackverse’19.



About IE NITK
IE NITK is one of the 6 exclusive clubs in NITK, aiming to increase technical exposure to all its members. Our previous Hackathon, Digital Hackathon which was conducted last year, sparked our ingenious students to transpire cutting edge solutions to various problem statements. Other events like SPARK, a small scale version of Ted talks and KOTH, a challenge filled series, sought to bring out the interest within our freshman.
