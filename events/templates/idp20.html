About Visionnaire

As students of engineering, we have generally been confined to the 4 walls of a classroom with scant opportunities to put our knowledge to the test. One of the main pain points that we have after leaving the institution is the disconnect between what we have learned in class and the work assigned to us. Visionnaire hopes to help you in overcoming this problem with the 'Industry Defined Problem' or IDP. Industry Defined Problem is platform for all the budding engineers out there to test their mettle by solving a real problem that occurs in the industry, provided to you by industry leaders.Here, you will get to show your skills in your field away from the confines of a classroom. It consists of 2 rounds - preliminary, wherein all submissions to the problem statement are reviewed and the best chosen, and the finals.

About NITK

National Institute of Technology Karnataka (NITK) is one of the premier engineering institutions in the country. Situated near Mangalore along the coast of the Arabian Sea, NITK is home to some of the brightest young minds of the country engaged in a wide array of disciplines. With a vibrant and robust student body, NITK has been the launchpad for people with great ideas that have grown to be very successful ventures including Practo, Perpule, SaveMonk among others. This year, we celebrate our Diamond Jubilee.

FAQs


  • Who is eligible to apply?

    Students enrolled in any college from any Department are welcome to apply.

  • How many team members?

    3 members in a team are required to apply.

  • Is there any registration fee?

    No. Registrations are completely free of cost.

  • Will accommodation be provided?

    Unfortunately, we won't be able to provide any accommodation to students.

  • Is a working model required?

    No, a simulation will be sufficient (if required).

  • Can team members be from different branches?

    Yes, team members can be of different branches.

  • Can you be a part of multiple problem statements?

    Yes you can take up multiple problem statements in Prelims. Though if you qualify for the finals you can only take ONE problem statement.

  • How many teams get selected for the finals?

    We are planning to select 6-8 teams across 4 problem statements for the final.

About IE NITK

Institution of Engineers, NITK Chapter, is one of the six exclusive clubs in NITK. We are divided by three core Special Interest Groups (SIGs) - Code, Gadget and Garage; but united by five auxiliary SIGs - Vriddhi, our social initiative, Script, our literary SIG, Robotics, Business and Media.

About SAE-NITK

SAE-NITK is a technical club for the automotive enthusiasts, dealing in projects related to the same. Students from all disciplines and backgrounds who have an interest in automobiles and emerging technologies get a platform to learn about the same through relevant projects and experience.

About FARC-NITK

Flying And Robotics Club (FARC) NITK is a technical club of motivated people who thrive to achieve excellence in Aeronautics and Robotics technology. Since its inception, in campus, the club has made its presence felt. Within FARC, we discuss future prospects in technology, develop organizational and leadership skills by working with like minded people to the best of our abilities.
